About

The Confederation Cup brings Canada's most talented high school debaters together to debate the issues that have shaped our country from before Confederation to the present day. Multiculturalism, the status of Quebec, justice for First Nations, medicare, conscription, oil sands development, and free trade with the US are just some of the great Canadian questions which will be argued out by Canada's next generation of leaders.

Organized and run by students, the Confederation Cup will also draw on an advisory group of distinguished educators, scholars, and community leaders for thematic advice and support. The Confederation Cup is an exciting convergence of youth leadership, public philanthropy, ‎and thoughtful reflection on core Canadian issues and values.
