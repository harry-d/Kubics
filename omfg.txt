                <li>Outline all proposition points</li>

            </ul>

            <p>
                6 minute constructive speech
            </p>

            <ul>
                <li>Outline all opposition points</li>
                <li>Introduce (at least) two points and fully develop them</li>
                <li>Respond to (refute) 1st Proposition's arguments</li>
            </ul>
        </div>
    </div>
