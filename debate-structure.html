Debate Structure

1st Proposition Speaker

6 minute constructive speech

  • Introduce the resolution
  • Outline all proposition points
  • Introduce (at least) two points and fully develop them

1st Opposition Speaker

6 minute constructive speech

  • Outline all opposition points
  • Introduce (at least) two points and fully develop them
  • Respond to (refute) 1st Proposition's arguments

2nd Proposition Speaker

6 minute constructive speech

  • Introduce (at least) one point and fully develop it
  • Respond to (refute) 1st Opposition's arguments
  • Rebuild 1st prop's arguments

2nd Opposition Speaker

6 minute constructive speech

  • Introduce (at least) one point and fully develop it
  • Respond to (refute) 2nd Prop's arguments
  • Rebuild 1st Opp's arguments

1st Proposition Speaker

(3 minute rebuttal/summary speech)

  • Remaining specific rebuttal and rebuilding
  • "Big picture" summary of round dealing with major issues to explain why your team has won
  • No new constructive ideas

1st Opposition Speaker

(3 minute rebuttal/summary speech)

  • Remaining specific rebuttal and rebuilding
  • "Big picture" summary of round dealing with major issues to explain why your team has won
  • No new constructive ideas

2nd Proposition Speaker

(3 minute rebuttal/summary speech)

  • Responsibilities as above, to be divided as partners deem fit
  • No new constructive ideas

2nd Opposition Speaker

(3 minute rebuttal/summary speech)

  • Responsibilities as above, to be divided as partners deem fit
  • No new constructive ideas
